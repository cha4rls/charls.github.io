Frequently Asked Questions


Can I cancel my booking/reservation?

You can cancel your booking/reservation by notifying us atleast 24 hours before the day of your booking without paying any fee. Failing to notify us less than 24 hours before your booking will cost you a fee of 150 AED if you want to proceed with the cancellation.


What are the requirements to proceed with renting a car?

You must be 18 years old or older to be able to rent a car in our company. The documents required are the following: Driver's License (valid for atleast 6 months), Valid Passport (for tourists), International Driver's License (for tourists), Emirates ID (for residents of UAE)


How do I know if my booking has been confirmed?

After completing the reservation form and submitting the required documents, we will send you a confirmation E-mail & SMS to notify you of your booking.


What payment options are available?

Online payment with credit/debit card & Onsite payment
